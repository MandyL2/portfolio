Mandy Luu (she/her) is a Toronto-based artist and developer who has always been a fan of the arts and video games. Her passion for these two things led her to explore the intersection of art and technology through game and web design/development as a Digital Futures student. Her innate curiosity has led her to explore and experiment with a variety of things within 2D art and games. On the side she uses art as an outlet to be funny, chaotic, and pay tribute to the things she loves, such as anime, visual novels, and fashion, injecting creative spunk into all her projects. Mandy aspires to find herself on a dynamic team and tackle big projects to do focused work, whether it be the next best story game or full-stack web development.
