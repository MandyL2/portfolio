Grandma Babushka and the Mystery of the Burnt Hut

A dark fantasy visual novel game based on the fairy tale "Vasilisa the Wise" Follow Grandma Babushka as she sets out to solve the spoopy mystery of what really happened to her family.

Two Kids Stacked in a Trenchcoat Pretending to be Adults

3D co-op game made in collaboration with UofT students for the Level Up Student Showcase 2022. Try to keep it together as you navigate the adult world and get into all sorts of mischief!

Lost & Found

A short and cozy 2D adventure game. Enjoy a relaxing nature walk in the woods. Remember to take time to enjoy the beautiful autumn scenery. You may notice some interesting items...

Undead Fortune

A fast-paced tabletop RPG game for 2 or more players. In a world overrun by zombies, you try to survive dad by dad as you look for a safe place to settle down. Put your improv skills to the test as you try to escape various sticky situations!
